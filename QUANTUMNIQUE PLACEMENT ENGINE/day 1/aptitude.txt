Question 1

A number increased by 20% becomes 120.
Find the original number.

Answer:

        original number = new number / 1 + percent increased
        
        = 120/1+0.20
        
        =100

Question 2

If the price of a laptop increases from ₹50,000 to ₹60,000,
what is the percentage increase?

Answer:

        original price - increased price

        50000 - 60000 = 10000

        10000 / 50000 = 0.2

        0.2 * 100 = 20 

        which is 20 percentage.

Question 3

A student scored 72 out of 90 in an exam.
Find the percentage score.

Answer:

        = 72 \ 90 * 100

        = 0.8 * 100

        = 80
        which is 80 % percentage.

Question 5

A shirt originally costs ₹800.
After a 25% discount, what is the final price?

Answer:
        original costs = ₹800
        
        800 * 25/100 = 200
        
        800 - 200 = 600 
        
        = ₹600
