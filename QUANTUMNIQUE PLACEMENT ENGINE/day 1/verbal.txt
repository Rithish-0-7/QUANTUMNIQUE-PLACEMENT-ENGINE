Reading Passage

Technology is transforming industries rapidly. Artificial Intelligence,
Machine Learning, and Automation are reshaping the way companies operate.
Businesses now depend heavily on data-driven decision making.

Questions

        1. What is the main idea of the passage?
        2. Which technologies are mentioned in the passage?
        3. Why are companies relying on data-driven decisions?

Answers:
        1)
        Main Idea: Technology is rapidly changing how industries 
        function and how companies make decisions.
        2)
        The passage specifically mentions three technologies:
            * Artificial Intelligence (AI)
            * Machine Learning
            * Automation
        3)
        Reason for Reliance: They are essential because 
        they have reshaped the way companies operate in 
        the modern era
